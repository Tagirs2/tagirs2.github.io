Thank you for taking the time to visit my page. I am the owner of 716 Microblading and Beauty (previously known as Divinity Holistic's and Beauty). My business was created with an emphasis on empowering women and providing a safe space where they could feel beautiful and confident.
Initially established in 2018, I started in the heart of the Elmwood Village and successfully introduced the ancient service of Yoni steaming to Western New York and the surrounding areas. 

 This service took off like wildfire, assisting women with so many reproductive issues that they previously were afraid to talk about or didn't have a safe space where their concerns were heard with a non judgemental listening ear.

I wanted to create that space. I wanted to create an oasis where women could be provided amazing one of kind services and experiences and could also meditate, concentrate, clear, and restart.
I am a University of Buffalo graduate and hold certifications for the ancient Hydrotherapy practice of Yoni steaming, and additionally the energy healing practice of Reiki and crystal healing therapy.

I am a New York State licensed permanent makeup artist and provide high-quality, frequently recommended cosmetic tattoo services including but not limited to Microblading, Ombre brows, and lip blush.



I regularly take new training to make sure I'm always up to date with the current permanent trends. All of my services are fully certified, insured, licensed and performed with the highest standard of care. We have surpassed all standards required by the NYS health department.
Being an artist at heart, spending many years creating beautiful portraits and detailed work, Microblading and cosmetic tattooing quickly became one of my loves. It just felt natural. I take great joy in my work. I love to see my clients' faces when they look in the mirror and see a new set of beautiful brows. I strive to make sure that everyone who walks through my door has a very memorable positive experience. Since opening, I have been featured in buffalo rising, the Griffin Newspaper, and other news outlets in which I have been allowed to provide additional knowledge about my services.
I look forward to meeting you one day, and I hope that you enjoy this little snippet about me.
