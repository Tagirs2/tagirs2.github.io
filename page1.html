What to expect

I want my clients to have a calm and wonderful experience when they walk into my Omaha studio. We start out with light paperwork and then discuss the look that you're going for. Microblading, Combination Brows, Powderbrows or Ombre. During the consultation, I will talk to you about what will be the best overall for your skin long term. I will use a topical numbing cream and than do a color match. Once we start the procedure I will continually keep you numb. The majority of my clients sleep during the entire process and wake up to their new look.
