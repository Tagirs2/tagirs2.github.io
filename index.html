Combination Brow

This is a permanent makeup service. If you have previous brow tattoo, please contact me prior to booking. This technique combines microblading (at the front of the brow) and shading throughout. While microblading creates the look of hair, manual shading deposits pigment to create a soft veil of color for a seamless blend. This service is best for specific skin types and clients who desire a natural yet defined brow. A touch-up may be necessary 6-12 weeks post initial treatment.

Book Now

Ombre Brow

This is a permanent makeup service. If you have previous brow tattoo, please contact me prior to booking. This technique creates a soft, ombré fade resulting in a natural ‘makeup look’. Great for all skin types including those with oily or mature skin. A touch up may be necessary 6-12 weeks post initial treatment.

Annual Touchup
