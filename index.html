Naomi's Beauty Bar

There is an old saying that your eyes are the primary feature of your face. Though that might be true to a degree, you can’t ignore their sister counterpart just above them, the eyebrows. In fact, eyebrows are such a focal point that it can throw off your whole look when they are not kept up. Due to that reality, you probably spend twice as long as you need to ensure that they are well-groomed.

Eyebrow procedures performed with our brow beauty bar

But here is the zinger; what if there was a better, faster, more luxurious way to get perfect brows without wasting time on getting ready every day? No more sparse brows, no more half smudged brows coming off during a night out, no more hassle, and the secret is through the power of permanent make-up nano blading, ombré powder, and combination brow services.

Nano-Blading Brows

Done by using an electric tool to draw hyper realistic fine hair like strokes that mimics real eyebrow hair. Giving you natural longer lasting brows. Can last up to 1.5 - 2 years

Ombre Powder Brows

Done by using an electric tool. This brow technique gives the illusion of having more brow hair than there is. Whether you like a natural powdery finish brow, or a bold powdery brow this set is completely tailored to you. This brow procedure can last you up to 1-2 years (after touch-up)

Combination Brows

This procedure involves both nanolading + ombre powder. Hair like strokes are drawn in the front of the brow or throughout the brow and shading is then added from the middle to the tail. Best for those who like the hair strokes and powder look. Can last up to 1-2 years. (after touch-up)
