OUR STORY

How it began

I've been in the beauty profession off and on for 2 decades now. I was drawn to the cosmetic tattooing side after a breast reduction that I had severe complications with at the age of 17. I woke up to a black necrotic breast and had to have multiple operations and tattooing was used to recreate an areola which was not done properly. As a natural artist I always wanted to go into this line of work because I knew how devastating this was to me and wanted to help other people by this incredible art. My life experience is what has launched me to find the best trainers for Microblading and Permanent Makeup and to continually seek out new ways to advance my skills and in return pass along my art and passion into something that brings happiness to my Omaha clientele.

— Appointment
