PROCESS

I believe you should have a clear vision of the direction the finished product is going prior to beginning. I will provide a full shaping consultation where we answer any questions, concerns, and goals – we then use that information to construct a customized shape based on what is achievable and perfect to your facial structure. The shape must be finalized and approved by you before we are permitted to begin the process of Microblading.
​
Once you approve the shape we then apply the first pass of microbladed strokes to the eyebrow we then allow you to make a final changes on the direction its going so that we can make any necessary adjustments while the opportunity exists. I value your opinion and believe the most satisfying outcomes are achieved through a guided process that ensures the expectations you have.
​
